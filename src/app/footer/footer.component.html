About

A Student Branch is the primary element of IEEE which offers the opportunity to meet and learn from fellow students, our mission, as well as faculty members and professionals in the field through activates as : -Technical and Non-Technical Sessions. -Seminars. -Workshops. -Competitions. -Tours and field trips. Also, Student Branches provide an opportunity for IEEE Student Members to begin networking in their areas of interest and future profession. There are over 3,000 Student Branches in over 100 countries, globally.
